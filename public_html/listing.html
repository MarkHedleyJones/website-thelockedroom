The Locked Room

A fully relocatable live escape game

Live escape room games are fast becoming the most popular activities in major centres around the world. [Auckland, Sydney, Melbourne, London, Florida, Glasgow, Brisbane, Calgary, + many more] The concept is simple: you are locked in a room where you must solve a series of puzzles to escape within a given time limit. Nine live escape games have opened around the country over the last eighteen months - three of which are based in Auckland. Take this opportunity to get into the live escape room market with this fully functional, plug-and-play business.

Container:

Shipping containers are easy to transport, are compact, and secure. The Locked Room has been built into a shipping container to promote ease of transport to wherever the new owner wishes to base this pop-up business. The container features:

  • Full insulation
  • Air conditioning
  • Built in ranch-slider (behind main container doors)
  • Front office
  • Lined walls, floor and ceiling
Once inside it is easy to forget you are inside a shipping container as the rooms are well lit with LED lighting and the walls are lined with tongue & groove style panels. A fire extinguisher has been installed and the container has a current electrical warrant of fitness. Photos of the inside show little of the room so we don’t give too much away; if you’re interested - we’d rather show you through in person.

The Room:

Themed around a 1950’s Cold War Bunker, the room contains an authentic Morse code machine, Bakelite telephones, and a telephone exchange. The exit door is surrounded with LED lights that change colour from red to green as the players complete the room, triggered by sensors placed within various puzzles. The sequence of puzzles has been carefully planned and tweaked to ensure the players have plenty to do inside the room, but not too much that they cannot finish in the allocated time of 60 minutes. Getting this right takes a lot of trial-and-error, which we have taken care of over the previous three months. Everyone we’ve put through the room have absolutely loved it - as can be seen by the feedback on TripAdvisor. Tourists with experience at other locked rooms consistently comment on the high quality of this particular room.

The Business:

The online booking system integrated into the website allows you to set your own hours easily. Going away for a weekend? Set those days as busy (from anywhere in the country) to prevent customers from booking a room on those days - it couldn’t be simpler. We’ve trialled the business for three months to check everything works as expected and to tweak the system. To be clear - this business has been designed from the start to be sold, leased, or franchised. With an mean group size of 2.6 people, the business takes an average of $82 per session. Running only two sessions each day yields $1148 in revenue per week, with plenty of room to grow. Fixed operating costs are less than $10 per week - excluding rent & power.
