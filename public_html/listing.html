The Locked Room

A fully relocatable live escape-room game

The Locked Room is a real-life escape-room game built into a 20' shipping container. The container is fully insulated, air conditioned, and is divided into two rooms: the 'escape' room and a front office. The front office is complete with ranch-slider, computer desk (with computer), and seating for clients. The room’s theme is "cold war espionage" and is furnished with authentic items relevant to the post war era. Items such as real Bakerlite telephones, morse-code machines, and telephone exchanges have had their internals reworked with appropriate electronics so as to be functional elements of the room. Sophisticated, behind the scenes, electronics are a major feature of this room providing feedback to both the players in the room and the operator in real-time. A unique puzzle stream (the sequence and specific interaction of puzzles that lead players to their end-goal) has been designed and fully tested to ensure trouble free operation. This game is currently pitched at a medium to difficult level but can be easily altered to create an easier game experience. The room is designed for three to four players, but can easily accommodate between two and five.

Currently, the game is located in Taupo, but is designed to be easily relocatable with no permanent fixtures necessary. Shipping containers are easy to move as well as being robust and able to be securely locked when not in use. The Locked Room comes with its own website, including an integrated booking management system, Facebook page and TripAdvisor listing - complete with all 100% positive reviews from our testing period.
