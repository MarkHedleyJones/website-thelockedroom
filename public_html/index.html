FREQUENTLY ASKED QUSTIONS:

Are we really trapped inside the room?

No. You will be shown the emergency exit before you start the locked room. You can leave at any time without having to solve puzzles.

Am I likely to feel claustrophobic?

Our room is built inside a shipping container, which is surprisingly large on the inside. The room is well lit and open plan so it is unlikely you will feel claustrophobic.

What age groups is the game suited to?

People of all ages are able to play. The material in the rooms is suitable for all ages. Some of the puzzles can be quite challenging so it is recommended that at least one person over 16 years of age be present.

Why do you prevent people from taking photos?

We ask you not to take photos inside the room because we want to keep it a secret. The mystery of what’s inside the room is part of the experience.
